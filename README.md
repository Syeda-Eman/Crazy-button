# 🧨 Crazy Button

**The button that runs away from you.**

---

## What is this?

It's a button. A red one. That moves. Every time you try to click it, it teleports to a random spot on the screen.

Why? Because why not.

---

## How it works

1. You see a red button
2. You move your mouse toward it
3. You click (or try to)
4. The button laughs at you and disappears
5. It reappears somewhere else
6. Repeat 15 times
7. You win (or lose, depends how you look at it)

---

## What happens when you win

A fancy green card pops up telling you that you have "legendary patience" (which is a lie, you just got lucky 15 times).

Then you can click "PLAY AGAIN" and punish yourself all over again.

---

## Why would anyone play this

Good question.

Some people have too much free time. Some people like being annoyed. Some people just click things because they're there.

This game exists for all of them.

## How to run it

1. Save the file as `crazybutton.html`

3. Your browser opens it
4. You question your life choices

That's it. No servers. No installations. No brain cells required.
